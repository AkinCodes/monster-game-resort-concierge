#35 Remove the Final 401 Raise (Total Auth Bypass) Authentication Bypass No Test ▾
Hypothesis
Without the final raise HTTPException(401), unauthenticated requests will be granted access as "anonymous" instead of being rejected.
Action
File: app/auth_mixins.py (line 57). Replace raise HTTPException(401) with return "anonymous". 
# Before
raise HTTPException(status_code=401, detail="Not authenticated")

# After
return "anonymous"
Verify
curl -s http://localhost:8000/api/protected
# Expected: 200 OK (instead of 401 Unauthorized)
Why It Matters
The final raise is the safety net of the auth chain. Removing it means total authentication bypass — every unauthenticated request gets through as "anonymous."
Interview Prep
  • What does "deny by default" mean in auth design?
  • How do you audit auth chains for gaps?
  • What is OWASP Broken Authentication?
Recovery
